About DHG Cleaning

DHG Cleaning is a professional cleaning company based in Montreal, Canada, dedicated to providing high-quality cleaning services and wholesale cleaning products.

We specialize in office cleaning, apartment cleaning, post-construction cleaning and commercial maintenance services. Our team focuses on professionalism, reliability and customer satisfaction.

We also provide wholesale cleaning products including toilet paper, brown paper, glass cleaner and other essential cleaning supplies for businesses, offices and commercial properties.

At DHG Cleaning, our mission is to provide clean, safe and professional environments while building long-term trust with every client.
